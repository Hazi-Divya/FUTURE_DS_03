# 📈 Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

### Power BI | Marketing Analytics | Business Intelligence

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the performance of marketing campaigns conducted by a Portuguese banking institution. The objective is to understand customer conversion behavior, identify factors influencing campaign success, and generate actionable business insights through interactive Power BI dashboards.

The analysis combines Python-based data preparation with Power BI visualizations to evaluate campaign effectiveness and customer conversion patterns.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Marketing campaigns generate a large number of leads, but only a small percentage convert into customers. Understanding customer behavior, campaign effectiveness, and conversion patterns can help organizations optimize marketing strategies and improve Return on Investment (ROI).

This project focuses on:

* Measuring conversion performance
* Identifying high-converting customer segments
* Evaluating campaign effectiveness
* Analyzing customer demographics and behavior
* Providing recommendations for future campaigns

## 🔍 Key Insights

✅ Overall campaign conversion rate is **11.7%**.

✅ Cellular contact methods achieved higher conversion rates than telephone and unknown methods.

✅ Customers with successful previous campaign interactions were significantly more likely to convert.

✅ Customers with tertiary education demonstrated stronger conversion performance.

✅ Conversion rates varied across occupations and demographic groups.

✅ Excessive customer contacts tended to reduce conversion effectiveness.

✅ The campaign experienced a high drop-off rate of **88.3%**, highlighting significant optimization opportunities.

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations

1. Prioritize customers with successful previous campaign interactions.
2. Increase the use of cellular communication channels.
3. Develop targeted campaigns for high-converting customer segments.
4. Optimize contact frequency to reduce customer fatigue.
5. Focus marketing resources on demographics with stronger conversion potential.
6. Personalize future campaigns using customer insights.
